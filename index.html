About Us

Rivoni Vila Recruitment is a vibrant, black female-owned recruitment agency based in Johannesburg, South Africa. We assist with employment both locally and internationally. Whether permanent, temporary, or contract employment—we are the people to call.

Our comprehensive recruitment services include permanent, temporary, and contract placements, along with professional recruitment processes that ensure the best fit for both clients and candidates.

  • Professionalism: High standards of service and ethics
  • Long-term Relationships: Focused on lasting partnerships
  • Client Satisfaction: Committed to excellence
  • All Sourcing Costs Covered: Advertising, sourcing, and screening costs are covered by the agency

Mission, Vision & Goal

Our Mission

To find and attract top talent so our client firms can have the best staff to advance their operations.

Our Vision

To establish a strong and diverse talent pipeline that meets the evolving needs of our clients and positions us as a leader in talent acquisition.

Our Goal

Increase efficiency, enhance the applicant experience, improve selection quality, and expand sourcing strategies.

Our Recruiting Process

We follow a proven, systematic approach to ensure the perfect match between candidates and employers. Our comprehensive process is designed to save you time while delivering exceptional results.

1
Initial Consultation

We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your specific requirements, company culture, and ideal candidate profile. This includes:

  • Detailed job specification analysis
  • Company culture and values assessment
  • Budget and timeline discussion
  • Success criteria definition
1-2 days
2
Strategic Sourcing

Our multi-channel sourcing strategy ensures we reach the best talent in the market:

  • Database search of pre-screened candidates
  • Targeted job board advertising
  • LinkedIn and professional network outreach
  • Industry-specific headhunting
  • Referral network activation
3-5 days
3
Comprehensive Screening

Rigorous screening process to ensure only the best candidates reach you:

  • Initial phone screening and qualification
  • Skills assessment and competency testing
  • Reference checks and background verification
  • Industry-specific consultant review
  • Cultural fit evaluation
2-3 days
4
Candidate Preparation

We prepare both candidates and clients for successful interviews:

  • Candidate interview coaching and preparation
  • Company and role briefing for candidates
  • Interview scheduling and coordination
  • Feedback collection and analysis
  • Salary negotiation support
1-2 days
5
Placement & Follow-up

We ensure a smooth transition and continued success:

  • Offer letter coordination and negotiation
  • Onboarding support and guidance
  • 30-day follow-up and feedback
  • 90-day performance check-in
  • Long-term relationship maintenance
Ongoing
